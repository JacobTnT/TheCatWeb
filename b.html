A Good Daily Rountine For Cats

When I mean by good, I don't mean like waiting 73 hours to give your cat food, or anything else.

What I mean is that this helps your cat live as long as possible

Let's get a move on.

Phase 1: Feeding

  • Normal Feeding Times: Feed your cat at the same times every day or, at least try if you are busy.

    • Phase 2: Playtime

    Play with your cat at least 15-20 minutes daily, or try if busy

    Phase 3: Grooming

    Regularly brush your cat to not have loose hair.

    Phase 4: Litter Box Care

    Clean the litter box daily and disinfect it weekly. Or try if busy

    Phase 5: Vet Care

    Schedule regular vet check-ups and vaccinations. Or try if busy

    Phase 6: Rest and Relaxing

    Before I start, I know most of you always need rest and relax, Because then you could feel grumpy and random.

  • Provide a quiet, comfy space for your cat to rest.
