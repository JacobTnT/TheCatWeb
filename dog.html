Part 1, Life Cycle

Puppies

Dogs start out as a puppy, It starts out blind and deaf. Puppies can't do anything and depend on their mother's milk. The puppy's life relies all on the mother After a month or less, Puppies become less reliant on their mother. Puppies start moving. Puppies start eating other foods. This stage lasts for the first year or less.

Teenager Years

On this stage, there's not really a age requirement as all dogs are different. Puberty now plays a role, The dog's attitude now changes as the hormones take action. Your dog now no longer has puppy fur and moved to adult fur.

Adult Years

On this stage your dog is trained and learning skills by YOU. Or a dog owner. Dogs are now more relaxed.

Old/Senior Years

After the adult phase, We enter the Senior Phase. The dog looks unactive but for smaller dogs, it may happen really late or never at all. Your dog gains weight faster from metabolism, Here is a link talking more about it Link
